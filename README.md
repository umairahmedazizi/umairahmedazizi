## Hi, I'm Umair Ahmed

I'm a BS-Economics student at IBA Karachi with a focus on data analysis and econometrics. I like
working a dataset all the way through, from cleaning and exploring it to modelling and writing up
what it actually says. I'm just as comfortable on the qualitative side, looking at marketing and
business strategy.

Most of what's here is coursework I've turned into proper, documented projects.

### Projects

- **[Retail Sales Impact Analysis](https://github.com/umairahmedazizi/amazon-retail-sales-eda)**
  Exploratory analysis and regression models on around 374,000 weekly sales records, looking at
  how holidays, promotions, and the economy affect sales. Python, pandas, scikit-learn, Plotly.

- **[Returns to Education in Pakistan](https://github.com/umairahmedazizi/returns-to-education-pakistan)**
  A Mincer wage regression on the Labour Force Survey 2024-25, estimating how education affects
  earnings. Stata.

- **[Shan Foods: Brand and Marketing Analysis](https://github.com/umairahmedazizi/shan-foods-marketing-analysis)**
  A brand and marketing-mix case study built on an interview with the company's marketing manager.

- **[Habib Oil Mills: The Organizational Buying Process](https://github.com/umairahmedazizi/habib-oil-mills-buying-process)**
  A look at how a large manufacturer makes its B2B purchasing decisions, theory against practice.
